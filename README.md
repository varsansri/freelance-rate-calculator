# Freelance Hourly Rate Calculator
> Stop undercharging. See your exact minimum hourly rate in 30 seconds.

**[Live Demo →](https://varsansri.github.io/freelance-rate-calculator)**

## What It Does
Enter your desired annual take-home pay, business expenses, vacation weeks, billable hours per day, and tax rate. The tool calculates three rate tiers: minimum (break-even), comfortable (30% buffer), and premium (2×). Shows daily rate, monthly revenue, and annual revenue at each tier.

## Who It's For
- Freelancers transitioning from full-time employment
- Consultants setting rates for the first time
- Remote workers going independent
- Designers, developers, writers, and coaches pricing services
- Anyone currently charging and wondering if they're underpriced
