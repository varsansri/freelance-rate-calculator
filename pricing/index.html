Project pricing tip: Estimate your hourly time × 1.5 for a project rate. The extra 50% covers scope creep, revisions, client communication, and the risk of underestimation. Never quote without a clear scope of work.

How to Raise Your Rates

  • Give existing clients 60-day notice of rate increases
  • Raise rates for new clients immediately — never mid-project
  • Raise by 20–30% per year in your first 3 years
  • Specialize in a niche — specialists always earn more than generalists
  • Build a portfolio of results, not just work samples
